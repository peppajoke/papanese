About Papanese

Papanese is a rare dialectal language with extremely limited documentation. Our neural translation model has been trained on the few surviving recordings and written fragments. Due to the language's highly contextual nature, translations may vary. Papanese is characterized by its direct emotional expression, frequent references to familial relationships, and distinctive interrogative structures.
